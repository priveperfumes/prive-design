Logo System

Two-tier system: the circle mark (navy circle + white PRIVÉ) is for contained icons — favicons, social avatars, standalone marks. The letters-only wordmark (white letterforms, transparent background) is the primary building block for everything else — headers, logos on backgrounds, wordmarks at any size. On dark or colored backgrounds the white letters render naturally; on white backgrounds, use CSS filter:invert(1) or inline SVG with fill="#192E49". Three SVG asset files live in assets/.

07

Brand Voice

Privé speaks like a knowledgeable friend recommending something special. Direct, warm, never performative. Spanish-first for all customer-facing content.

Personality traits

Confident
not arrogant
We know our products. We don't need to prove it with logos everywhere or excessive superlatives.
Warm
not casual
Every interaction feels personal, like a recommendation from someone who genuinely understands fragrance.
Masculine-leaning
not exclusionary
The navy + orange palette has masculine energy, but warmth keeps it open. A well-designed men's store that women also love browsing.
Premium
not expensive
We signal quality through restraint and detail. Whitespace is our luxury signal, not gold or price flashing.

Tone spectrum

Privé
Formal Casual

Do's and don'ts

Do — Mood over notes

"La hora azul, antes de que alguien encienda una luz."

Don't — Generic note listing

"Bergamota, pachulí, sándalo."

Do — Direct, respectful

"Nuevo en tienda. Pruébalo antes que nadie."

Don't — Hashtag spam

"#Luxury #Perfume #Honduras #NewArrival #FragranceLovers"

Do — Spanish-first

"Disponible para entrega inmediata en Tegucigalpa."

Don't — AI slop

"Unleash your inner confidence with this must-have fragrance!"

Spanish-first rule

Customer-facing: Always Spanish. Product descriptions, social media, email, WhatsApp, all marketing.

Internal: English for code, docs, and developer tools.

Price format: L. X,XXX (period for thousands, comma for decimals in amounts under 1)

Max emojis: 2 per asset. Zero in editorial contexts.
