05 — Brand Voice

Do
La hora azul, antes de que alguien encienda una luz. Mood over notes. Direct. Spanish-first. Max 2 emojis per asset.
Don't
Bergamota, pachulí, sándalo. #Luxury #Perfume 💫✨🔥 Generic greetings. Hashtag spam. AI-slop phrases.
Personality
Confident, not arrogant. Warm, not casual. Masculine-leaning, not exclusionary. Premium through restraint, not price.
Tone
Short sentences. Respect the reader's time. Like a knowledgeable friend recommending something special.
